The leftovers and getting rid of them.

Our office is managed by a specialized team of lawyers and advisors who oversee the evaluation and liquidation of estates, the execution of testators' wills and the fulfillment of trust conditions. Debt collection.

We are distinguished by our commitment and responsibility in dealing with clients, ensuring continuous communication and achieving payment results.

Arbitration

Arbitration is a method of resolving disputes by agreeing to refer the dispute between parties to one or more neutral parties called arbitrators, who settle the dispute by issuing a binding and enforceable arbitration award instead of being decided by the court.
